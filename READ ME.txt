#Food_Blog
Hello, welcome to our website. 
We have created a personal blog on the topic called "Food Blog".
In this website, there are reviews posted about the food of renowned
restaurents of Ballari. Also, people can share their reviews in our
website.
We used HTML, CSS and Javascript as frontend.
MongoDB and Nodejs are used for backend. 


Installation:
1. MongoDB compass for databse
2. Nodejs for server connection
   In nodejs, initially install node.
   Then, install "npm install nodemon" and "npm install express"
   modules.

In our website, there is a option for people to subscribe to our 
website. A person must enter their email-id with "@gmail.com" and
press subscribe button. Then, their email will be stored in the database.
